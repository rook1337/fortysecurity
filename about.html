About Forty Security

Who We Are

Forty Security is a forward-thinking cybersecurity company dedicated to protecting organizations against modern and evolving threats. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical defense, we provide specialized expertise across multiple security domains.

We are a group of young, passionate minds who understand the latest cybersecurity trends. That’s why we focus on emerging technologies like Drone Security and AI/LLM Security Testing — areas that are evolving rapidly and must be secured from attackers before they become major targets.

Our team includes some of the top bug bounty hunters and penetration testers in the world, with acknowledgments from companies like Apple, Microsoft, the U.S. Department of Defense, Zomato, and Twitter (X). We also have highly certified professionals holding OSCP, OSWE, and other elite certifications, ensuring industry-grade expertise.

Over time, we’ve successfully served clients across industries such as Government Ministries, Oil & Energy, Banking, Corporate Enterprises, Money Exchange Companies, and Insurance. This wide exposure allows us to tailor solutions to different critical environments while maintaining the highest standards of security.

At Forty Security, our mission is simple: empower businesses to stay secure, resilient, and compliant in the digital age. We proudly follow a “Make in India” approach, building world-class cybersecurity solutions from the ground up.
